SERVICES

MJK Beauty provides a long list of salon services that will have women looking fabulous. Women can treat themselves to just a haircut or they can also choose from any of our expert services. Our philosophy is customised creativity. Our innovative team of stylists will create a look that caters for you and your lifestyle.

HAIR

When it comes to looking good we know it’s all about the hair. We don’t just cut hair we create a style based on you.

READ MORE
HAIR

The key to having healthy, voluminous hair is having a healthy body. We constantly educate our clients on the best practices to use by also demonstrating outstanding style without damaging their hair. In fact, we aim to heal previously damaged hair and achieve many styles without using thermal heat. While continuously redefining the standards of appearance, we are constantly promoting the importance of having a healthy lifestyle.

MAKE UP

We believe every woman has a beauty queen inside her. Makeup should enhance natural beauty and also build your confidence.

READ MORE
MAKE UP

Makeup is not intended to mask your face, but to bring out key attributes you naturally possess. While enhancing your greatest features, we educate you on the best practices of having and maintaining healthy skin; resulting in a gorgeous presence that is hard to ignore. We also provide an all natural face detox that has immediate results.

NAILS

Your hands say a lot about you, so make sure they are saying the right things. We think your hands should be saying you’re a beautiful, classy lady.

NAILS

Having great looking nails is a requirement; they can be the body’s natural accessory. From traditional looks to styles that are outlandishly chic, we raise the bar with every opportunity. Like hair and skin, the key to beautiful nails also stems from good health. Keeping you informed about some of the best ways to take care of yourself is our highest priority.

ABOUT US

In a market of high-end salons and posh boutiques MJK Beauty has taken their combined 12 years of industry experience to create a new take on the old mom and pop salon.
MJK Beauty is a place that takes you from feeling comfortable when you walk in the door, to confident by the time you leave.
You’ll find an extremely talented and experienced staff of stylists inspired by your individuality and not the latest trend.

If you’re looking for a salon to bring your ideas to, or one where you need to find some – MJK Beauty is your style.
